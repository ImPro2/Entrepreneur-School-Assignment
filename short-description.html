The Bakker Bartin sells Soda Bread! Soda Bread is a form of bread developed by the Irish. It kind of looks like a hot-cross bun, but much bigger (and of course, much more delicious). It is mainly composed of flour, sugar, baking soda and powder, and buttermilk. Why did I choose to sell? Mostly because I love bread and I feel selfish keeping it to myself - so I decided to sell it so that you don't need to make it!
